EasyIDE

EasyIDE is a versatile Integrated Development Environment designed to cater to different user needs. Here are the versions planned for release:

  • Free Versions:
    • EasyIDE Student: Tailored for students with age-specific features, numerous examples, and simple commands.
    • EasyIDE Personal: General-purpose IDE for individual developers.
    • EasyIDE Enterprise: Suitable for small startup software companies with teams of 1-10 employees.
  • Paid Versions:
    • EasyIDE Student: ₹50 (approx. $0.60). More default packages and themes.
    • EasyIDE Personal: ₹120 (approx. $1.50). Includes advanced features like enhanced error handling, syntax highlighting, and line completion.
    • EasyIDE Enterprise: ₹250 (approx. $3.00). Designed for larger companies with 50+ employees, offering more robust tools and support.
